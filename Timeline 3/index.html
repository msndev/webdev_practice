Our Story

2011

ISTE conducted 5 events in the cultural fest Riviera'11 and 9 events in the technical fest graVITas'11. The National Summit Talk @ Green India (TAGI) was also organised by us on a much larger scale attracting students interested in discussing our role in saving the planet of disastrous consequences.

2012

ISTE conducted 2 events in the cultural fest Riviera'12 and 10 events in the technical fest graVITas'12. ISTE-VITU won the "Best Students' Chapter Award" at ISTE National Students' Convention this year.

2013

ISTE conducted 4 events in the cultural fest Riviera'13 and 13 events in the technical fest graVITas'13. We also started various outreach programs like Teach For Vellore and IC Bank.

2014

ISTE conducted 4 events in the cultural fest Riviera'14 and 12 events in the technical fest graVITas'14. This year saw ISTE conducting a series of workshops called Tech League and Tronix League.

2015

ISTE conducted 3 events in the cultural fest Riviera'15 and 14 events in the technical fest graVITas'15. ISTE took its members on an Industrial Visit to Daimler Chennai, hence introduced students to the Industrial World this year.

2016

ISTE conducted 2 events in the cultural fest Riviera'16 and 10 events in the technical fest graVITas '16. ISTE-VITU won the "Best Students' Chapter Award" at ISTE Students' Convention this year.

2017

In 2017, ISTE conducted 8 events during graVITas and 3 events during Riviera. ISTE also conducted a tech fest HORIZON 2017 focussing on various fields like web development, image processing, competitive coding and graphic designing.
